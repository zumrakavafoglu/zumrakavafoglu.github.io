I am currently an assistant professor at Hacetepe University Institute of Informatics. I received my M.Sc and Ph.D. degrees from Hacettepe University Department of Mathematics, by conducting interdisciplinary research studies covering Computer Science topics. During my Ph.D, I worked as a visiting researcher at Utrecht University(Netherlands), Department of Information and Computing Sciences from December 2014 to June 2015 . My current research interests consist of computer graphics and animation, especially physics-based and data-driven human animation as well as machine learning and optimization methods for computer animation.
